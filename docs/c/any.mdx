# Career Any% route

The perfect route to start and master your speedrunning journey in Palmont City.

## Prologue

After pressing `Start Career`, a skippable cutscene will play, followed by a short unskippable one. Once you gain control of your infamous BMW M3, your camera will be set to `Far` automatically. You want to change your camera to `Hood` cam. Then, insta NOS and drive as fast as possible to the ending trigger. You want to downshift to engine brake in the corners to retain a high amount of grip, in turn letting you keep more speed. **Speedbreaker usage is not recommended on this event.**  
Two ending cutscenes will then play and you can skip the second one. You will be asked to choose a new car : **choose the Camaro SS** and then press `Start` to avoid doing the Test Drive.

## Tutorial

After choosing your new car, you have to do a tutorial, presenting you new stuff : **Crew members and 3 abilites**. However, you want to complete this tutorial as fast as possible but explaining how to do it in writing is complicated. A video example is way better to explain then.

## Post-tutorial chase

After the tutorial, an unskippable cutscene will play. Once you get control back of your car, you will be chased by 3 cops. The goal here is to escape them with the right timing and to do that you can use a new feature the game brings : **rolling pursuit breakers** like trailers with loose cargo. Once escaped, head to the Safe House to move on to the next chapter.

> After escaping, you will get an SMS from Neville telling you to meet him at the Safe House. To avoid it, the chase time has to be at least 22 seconds long approximatively to get the right placement in the map to drive to the Safe House without triggering the SMS.

Upgrade the following parts :

|      Package      |   Parts   |
|:-----------------:|:---------:|
|   Street Engine   | 3 / 3 / 3 |
| Street Suspension | 3 / 2 / 3 |
|      Pro NOS      | 1 / 1 / 1 |

After **<font color= "#ff0000">Race 7</font>**, upgrade *Pro Engine 3 / 3 / 3*.

Once you challenged Angie and won the first boss race, you will face her in a Canyon race. Instead of doing both rounds, you can win the event by **overtaking her** on the first one. There are 2 ways of overtaking Angie but a video is worth a thousand words.

## Downtown Palmont / Fortuna chapter (<font color="#18d73a">Kenji</font> / <font color= "#0080fd">Wolf</font>)

<img src="/nfsd-recreation/img/cropped_DownPal_Fortuna.png" alt="DownPal-Fortuna territory route" />

After **<font color= "#ff0000">Race 18</font>**, go to a Car Lot your mouse is the closest of and buy the Elise.  
Upgrade the following parts :

|      Package      |   Parts   |
|:-----------------:|:---------:|
|     Pro Engine    | 3 / 3 / 3 |
| Street Suspension | 3 / 2 / 3 |
|     Street NOS    | 1 / 1 / 1 |
|    Street Tires   |     2     |
|    Street Turbo   | 3 / 3 / 2 |

Start **<font color= "#ff0000">Race 19</font>** from the Safe House.  
Once done, upgrade *Pro NOS 3 / 3 / 3*.

Once you challenged Kenji (**Race 28**) and won the first boss race, you will face her in a Canyon race. Instead of doing both rounds, you can win the event by **overtaking him** easily on the first one.  
After beating him, skip the markers.

Start **<font color= "#ff0000">Race 31</font>** from **Kenji's Safe House**.  
Like Kenji, you can overtake Wolf easily during his Canyon race.

Once Worf defeated, skip the markers. Then, you will be asked to meet Darius in Downtown Palmont. Go meet him and hire Nikki.

**Race 47** is the last event of the run. It consists of 4 races : 2 against Angie, Kenji and Wolf then 2 against Darius including a Canyon race.

Regarding the Canyon race against Darius, you can overtake him but it is very tricky and will need a lot of practice. There are 2 ways of overtaking him :

* on **Attempt 0**, the hard way or
* on **Attempt 1**, the safer way.

Why this type of names ? If you insta-restart the Canyon race, you will spawn further back (hence ***Attempt 1***) and will get more room to overtake Darius.
