# Career Any% route

The perfect route to start and master your speedrunning journey in Palmont City.

## Prologue

An annoying cutscene will play after pressing `Start Career` but you can skip it. Then, a unskippable short one will play. Once you gain control of your infamous BMW M3, your camera will be set to `Far` automatically. You want to change your camera to `Hood` cam. Then, insta NOS and drive as fast as possible to the ending trigger. You might downshift a lot to gain your speed on corners. **The speedbraker usage is not recommended on this event**.  
Two ending cutscenes will then play and you can skip them. You will be asked to choose a new car : **choose the Camaro SS** and then press `Start` to avoid doing the tutorial race.
