# Least Wanted Any% route

An optimized route that demands mastery, consistency, and flawless execution.  
**This route uses the [Expert Career Any% route style](https://xicecreaam.github.io/nfsd-recreation/mw05/any/expert-any/), meaning everything will be shorten.**

Before starting your journey, there is something important to note : your **cash balance**. At Kaze, you want to switch to the Elise to save time. Otherwise, it requires a lot of money to upgrade. Markers at the end of each boss are random and creates few scenarios, hence the need to draw a table.

To not encounter any issue during a run, you need to **get Sonny's car and the 10k cash marker**, otherwise you won't get enough cash on Kaze to upgrade the Elise. Not having any cash or car markers basically means a <font color="red">**RESET**</font>.

## Prologue

~~I sure hope you like watching the cutscene.~~  
Complete the prologue. After Rog circuit, go back to *SH* and enter career to skip the Razor cutscene.  
Buy Golf and drive to *SH*. Pressing the `Reset` button while in the Loading screen can save you time, up to 0.3-0.4 second.

## Sonny

Do *Race 1*. Then, turn around and go to the *Performance Shop*.  
Upgrade *Pro Engine* and install `Reaper` spoiler.  
Do *Race 2*. Don't forget the *Aero* `+5` tune at the stadium.  
Do *Race 3* then challenge Sonny. Choose *2 random markers*.
