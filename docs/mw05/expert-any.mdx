# Expert Career Any% route

An ultra-optimized route that demands mastery, consistency, and flawless execution.  
**From now on, a lot of things won't be explained. It will be assumed that you already got informations, tricks and useful strats in mind.**

## Prologue

Lapglitch **C14**, **C1**, **C2** and **C3**.

~~I sure hope you like watching the cutscene.~~  
Complete the prologue. After Rog circuit, go back to *SH* and enter career to skip the Razor cutscene.  
Buy Golf and drive to *SH*.

## Sonny

Do *Race 1*. Then, turn around and go to the *Performance Shop*.  
Upgrade *Pro Engine* and install `Reaper` spoiler.  
Do <font color="#19bf00">*Race 2*</font>. Don't forget the *Aero* tune at the stadium. If you get cops, kill on of them after finishing the race, gather 4 cops and go to the stadium. Go for *Cam 1* after killing them.  
If you don't get cops, <font color="red">**RESET**</font>. At this point, you lose time by doing a manual chase.  
Do *Race 3* then challenge Sonny. Choose *2 random markers*.

## Taz

* **Cash marker scenario**

    Upgrade *Race Trans*, *Pro Susp* and *Pro NOS*.  
    Do *Cam 1*.  
    Start *Race 4*. After the two S corners, tune *Handling* to `-3` or `-1` *(up to your preference)*, *Ride Height* to `+5` and *NOS* to `+2`.  
    Start <font color="#19bf00">*Race 3*</font>. If you get cops, gather 3 cops and kill them at the stadium and escape. A 4th cop can spawn at around 12 seconds whithin the chase. If so, wait for him and kill the wave.  
    If you don't get cops, you can do a Taz cure chase but i suggest to <font color="red">**RESET**</font>.  
    Lapglitch **C4** and **C5**.  
    Do *Cam 2*.  
    Do *Race 6* and *Race 1* then challenge Taz.  
    **Boss skip !** Choose the *Susp junkman marker* and *1 random marker*.

* **Car marker only scenario**

    Sell Sonny's car and lapglitch **C4** and **C5**.
    Upgrade *Race Trans*, *Pro Susp* and *Pro NOS*. Reset your car and do *Cam 2*.  
    Start *Race 4*. After the two S corners, tune *Handling* to `-3` or `-1` *(up to your preference)*, *Ride Height* to `+5` and *NOS* to `+2`.  
    Start <font color="#19bf00">*Race 3*</font>. If you get cops, gather 3 cops and kill them at the stadium and escape. A 4th cop can spawn at around 12 seconds whithin the chase. If so, wait for him and kill the wave.  
    If you don't get cops, you can do a Taz cure chase but i suggest to <font color="red">**RESET**</font>.  
    Do *Cam 1*.  
    Do *Race 6* and *Race 1* then challenge Taz.  
    **Boss skip !** Choose the *Susp junkman marker* and *1 random marker*.

## Izzy

Upgrade *Pro Tires* and *Junkman Susp*.  
Do *Cam 2*.  
Start <font color="#19bf00">*Race 7*</font>.

* You can get a block during this short chase, if so, farm it ASAP before escaping.
* Don't make the chase last forever above 4 minutes. If you triggered your backup early, you can do a 4:00 flat chase.
* If you don't get cops, <font color="red">**RESET**</font>. At this point, you lose time by doing a manual chase.

Lapglitch **C6** and start *Race 2* from *SH*.

* Wait for 5 cops to spawn and kill them at [the gas station behind you](https://ibb.co/nNryYmKY) in 40 - 50 seconds. It is possible to do a sub 40 chase here if the spawn is good.
* You can skip some cameras, depending on how much bounty you farm during the first chase. The following table resumes how many cam(s) you can skip.

|   ฿   |  Cam skip ? |
|:-----:|:-----------:|
|  10k  | no cam skip |
|  15k  | 1 cam skip  |
|  20k  | 2 cams skip |
|  26k  | 3 cams skip |

<span style={{ fontSize: '1.25rem', fontWeight: 'bold' }}> If yes, cams should be skipped in this order : **Vic Cam 1** → **Cam 1** → **Vic Cam 2** </span>

Start *Race 1*. If you are in the situation where you cannot skip any cam, hit Vic *Cam 2*. Then, start Vic *Race 1*. Quit to free roam, turn around and go for *Cam 1*.  
Do *Race 1*, *Race 5* and challenge Izzy.  
**Boss skip !** Choose the *Turbo junkman marker* and *1 random marker*.

## Baron

Upgrade *Pro Eng*, *Pro Trans*^1^, *Super Pro Susp* and *Super Pro NOS*.

> |:-----------------------------:|:----------------------------:|:---------------------------:|:---------------------------------------:|
> |              Yes              |              No              |             Yes             |                   Yes                   |
> |               No              |              Yes             |             Yes             |                   Yes                   |
> |               No              |              Yes             |              No             |                    No                   |

Do *Race 7* and *Race 5*.  
Start a manual chase. Do the cooldown trick one time.

* Don't waste too much time in this chase. A 5:30-ish one is ok but the faster you get the better it is.
* The bounty requirement for this chase depends on the amount of roadblocks you will get.

| Blocks |  ฿   |
|:------:|:----:|
| 0 - 1  | 147k |
| 2 - 3  | 117k |
| ≥ 4    | 90k  |

After the chase, lapglitch **KO6**.  
Do *Race 1*.  
Do *Race 2*.  
Do *Race 8*. If you got less than 4 blocks, go hit *Cam 3*. Otherwise skip it and challenge Baron.  
**Boss skip !** Choose the *Susp junkman marker* and *1 random marker*.

## Jewels

No upgrades here.  
Do *Cam 2*.  
Do *Race 3*. Hit Earl *Cam 2* afterwards.  
Start <font color="#19bf00">*Race 4*</font>.

* You can do a sub 6 chase if you have a good overshoot on Baron and if the bounty is really good on this chase.
* The bounty requirement is an exact amount depending on whether you got 4 blocks on Baron, whether you get 6 blocks during this chase, whether you gather both 4 AND 6 blocks milestones or nothing at all.

|       —      | 6 mins | sub 6 | sub 6 + QE |
|:------------:|:------:|:-----:|:----------:|
|     none     |  368k  |  404k |    358k    |
|   4 blocks   |  333k  |  369k |    323k    |
|   6 blocks   |  322k  |  358k |    312k    |
| 4 + 6 blocks |  287k  |  323k |    277k    |

* **If you don't get cops on *Race 4*, start *Race 5* from *SH*.**
* If you still don't get cops on this one, <font color="red">**RESET**</font>. You could risk it, but at this point, you would lose time by doing a manual chase.

|   If...   | What do to |
|:---------:|:----------:|
| ฿ ≥ 1156k |    Cam 3   |
| ฿ ≥ 1134k |     QE     |
| ฿ ≥ 1110k | Cam 3 + QE |

Lapglitch **KO8**.  
Do *Race 2*.  
If your chase was sub 6, do *Cam 3*, otherwise skip it.
Do *Race 1* and *Race 6*.  
If you are short on bounty, do a *QE*.
Then challenge Jewels. Complete both *Boss races*.  
**Boss skip !** Choose the *Performance marker*, *1 random marker*
