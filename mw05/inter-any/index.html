Intermediate Career Any% guide

A route designed for those who have mastered the basics and want to optimize their runs.

Prerequisite before starting‚Äã

If you discover this route, you are surely a player who is now more comfortable with the game. If this is not the case, you must go through the "Beginners route".

  • From now on, every Quick Escape is started from Bounty 1. Every manual chase is started from Bounty 2.
  • Unless otherwise stated, every race will be started from free roam.

Thanks to the High World Details, you will be able to trigger a cop speech earlier than usual on some races. This means you can encounter cops during them which results in a time save. See below an example of how to trigger a cop speech early.

Cooldown trick‚Äã

In the game, you have something called Cop spawn rate. It means that after an exact amount of time, cops are spawning and it depends on which condition (üî•) you are. This spawn rate is quickly maxed out after time but you can max it out way faster and easily. How does it work ? By going in and out of cooldown, you somehow force the cop spawn to its maximum.

Prologue‚Äã

Start the run by lapglitching C14, C1, C2 and C3. You can press LB/L1 to lapglitch C14 faster, depending if you're using an XInput or a DSX controller. Then, RB/R1 or LB/L1 twice to lapglitch C1, C2 and C3.

Intermediate / Expert first lap glitch example

Cutscene time...... Once you gain control of the M3, full NOS and reach the Mia call trigger.
Complete Ronnie and Bull sprints + Rog circuit as usual. Usually, after this race you have a cutscene involving Razor and you but you can skip this cutscene very easily. When you cross the finish line, restart the race then open the menu, quit back to main menu and go into career again. This glitch will skip the cutscene and will send you directly to Razor sprint.
Complete Razor sprint as usual. Then, buy the Golf (4) and go to SH.

Sonny‚Äã

Do Race 1 from the Safe house. After finishing the race, turn around and go to the performance shop. Install any spoiler you want and Race Engine.
Start Race 2 from free roam. Once at the stadium, at around 48% of the race, press start and tune Aero to +5. If you trigger the cops in the race, finish the race without losing them. Then, you need to escort the cops and head to the stadium by taking this path.
If you don't get cops during the race, start a manual chase.
Do Cam 1 (130 km/h | 80 mph).
Do Race 3 then challenge Sonny. Complete both Boss races.
Don't boss skip ! Just spam your Confirm button (A or X or with the mouse). Choose 2 random markers and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Taz‚Äã

Before continuing, it is important to note which marker you got at Sonny. Two scenarios are possible here :

  • Scenario 1 ‚Äî You got the cash marker.

    Upgrade Race Transmission, Pro Suspension and Pro NOS.
    Do Cam 1 (150 km/h | 93 mph).
    Tune Handling to -1, Ride Height to +5 and NOS to +2.
    Start Race 3. If you trigger the cops in the race, finish the race without losing them. Then, continue forwards to the stadium and kill everybody chasing you with the stadium pursuit breaker. Escape on the same hiding spot as on Sonny.
    Do Cam 2 (145 km/h | 90 mph) after the chase.
    If you don't get cops during Race 3, start a manual chase. You would also hit Cam 2 (145 km/h | 90 mph) during the chase. Go back to SH and lapglitch C4 and C5.
    Then start Race 1 from the Safe house.
    Do Race 4 and Race 6 then challenge Taz. Complete both Boss races.
    Boss skip ! Choose the Performance marker, 1 random marker and confirm to move on to the next boss.

  • Scenario 2 ‚Äî You got Sonny's car only.

    Go back to SH, sell Sonny's car and lapglitch C4 and C5.
    Quit to free roam, go to the performance shop to your right and upgrade Race Transmission, Pro Suspension and Pro NOS.
    Do Cam 1 (150 km/h | 93 mph).
    Tune Handling to -1, Ride Height to +5 and NOS to +2.
    Start Race 3. If you trigger the cops in the race, finish the race without losing them. Then, continue forwards to the stadium and kill everybody chasing you with the stadium pursuit breaker. Escape on the same hiding spot as on Sonny.
    Do Cam 2 (145 km/h | 90 mph) after the chase.
    If you don't get cops during Race 3, start a manual chase. You would also hit Cam 2 (145 km/h | 90 mph) during the chase.
    Do Race 1, Race 4 and Race 6 then challenge Taz. Complete both Boss races.
    Boss skip ! Choose the Performance marker, 1 random marker and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Izzy‚Äã

Upgrade Pro Tires and Junkman Suspension.
Do Cam 2 (195 km/h | 121 mph). Start Race 7.

  • Tag 8 cops.
  • The chase length is between 4:00 and 4:20.
  • The bounty requirement for this chase is 10k minimum. You should get at least 15k at the end of the chase anyways.

If you don't trigger the cops during the race, start a manual chase. Try to trigger a backup as soon as possible.

Once escaped, go back to SH and lapglitch C6.
Then, start Race 2 from SH.

  • Gather 5 cops and drive to the gas station behind you in Gray Point.

If you don't trigger the cops during the race, start a manual chase from Bounty 2. Then gather 5 cops as soon as possible and kill them at the closest pursuit breaker.
Do Race 1. Then hit Vic Cam 2 (170 km/h | 105 mph) after the race on your left.
Start Vic Race 1 from the Race menu. Once it loads, quit to free roam, turn around and go for Cam 1 (185 km/h | 115 mph).
Do Race 5. Go back to the Safe house, lapglitch circuit 7 and challenge Izzy. Complete both Boss races.
Boss skip ! You can skip the markers section by clicking just about right in the right corner, as if you were boss skipping.

Baron‚Äã

Upgrade Pro Engine, Super Pro Suspension and Super Pro NOS.
Do Race 7 and Race 5.
Start a manual chase. Do the cooldown trick one time. The chase length has to be 5 minutes minimum.

  • The bounty requirement for this chase depends on the amount of roadblocks you will get.
Blocks‡∏ø
0 - 1147k
2 - 3117k

If you get at least 4 blocks during the chase, you will not need to hit Cam 3 (225 km/h |140 mph) as you already completed 4 milestones. But if you get less than 4 blocks, you will need to hit it anyways. There are 2 ways to hit Cam 3 :

  • a safe way : from Race 2 ;
  • a hard way : from Race 8.

If you feel confident, choose the hard way but it requires practice since the cam speed requirement is 225 km/h.
I suggest to stick to the safe way if you are trying this route for the first time.

After the chase, go back to SH and lapglitch KO6.
Go back to free roam and start Race 1.
Do Race 2.
Do Race 8.
No matter where you hit Cam 3, challenge Baron. Complete both Boss races.
Boss skip ! Choose the Performance marker, 1 random marker and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Jewels‚Äã

Don't upgrade here.
Do Cam 2 (238 km/h | 148 mph).
Do Race 3. Hit Earl Cam 2 (220 km/h | 137 mph) afterwards.
Start Race 4. If you trigger the cops in the race, finish the race without losing them. The chase length has to be 6 minutes minimum. You would also tag 18 cops and commit 5 infractions to complete 3 milestones.

  • The bounty requirement is an exact amount depending on whether you got 4 blocks on Baron, whether you get 6 blocks during this chase, whether you gather both 4 AND 6 blocks milestones or nothing at all.

Ming‚Äã

Upgrade Super Pro Engine, Super Pro Transmission, Ultimate Tires and Junkman Transmission.
Start a manual chase.

  • The chase length has to be between 7:00 and 8:00.
  • Tag 22 cops and avoid 4 spikes to complete 3 milestones.
  • Regarding the 10 roadblocks milestone, it is not necessary to do but can be extra bounty for the next chapter.
  • The bounty requirement for this chase is not an exact amount. Try to get as much as bounty as you can in due time. The minimum is 700k.

Go back to SH and lapglitch KO11.
Tune Handling to 0 or +1 (depending on what you prefer) and start Race 7 from free roam.
Usually, +1 makes the Golf easier to drive.

After the race, continue forwards to hit Cam 1 (180 km/h | 112 mph).
Do Race 1, Race 4, Race 6, Race 8, Race 9 and Race 10.
Before challenging Ming, lapglitch circuit 16. The region should be set to Rosewood College and you will need to switch either to All Regions or City. All Regions = C16 or City = C2.
Challenge Ming and complete both Boss races.
Boss skip ! Choose 2 random markers and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Do Cam 3 (245 km/h | 152 mph).
Start a manual chase.

  • Earn 150k CTS and commit 6 infractions.

With this 2300k base, you will need 912k bounty during JV chase, as fast as possible. However, this amount can be drastically reduced if the bounty grinding during Ming chase was good. Bull‚Äã

Upgrade Ultimate Engine and Junkman Engine.
Do Cam 1 (313 km/h | 194 mph). Go hit Cam 3 (266 km/h | 165 mph) afterwards, on the right.
Go back to the Safe house and lapglitch circuit 23.
Do Race 1. After the finishline, continue forwards and go hit Cam 2 (220 km/h | 137 mph).
Do Race 2, Race 6 and Race 8.
Start a manual chase.

  • Don't do any cooldown trick here.
  • Earn 200k cost to state and avoid 8 spikes.
  • It is possible that you get 12 roadblocks before getting 8 spikes, depending on your RNG luck. If so, don't bother avoiding 8 spikes.
  • The bounty requirement here is 2700k minimum. The more bounty you have at this point will be useful for the next chapter.

Do Race 9, Race 10, Race 11 and Race 12.
Challenge Bull and complete the 3 Boss races.
Don't boss skip ! You can skip the markers section by clicking just about right in the right corner, as if you were boss skipping.

Razor‚Äã

Don't upgrade here since there is nothing to upgrade left.
Do Cam 3 (320 km/h | 198 mph). After getting it, turn left and hit Cam 2 (225 km/h | 139 mph).
Do Race 10 and after the finishline, turn immediately on the left way to hit Ronnie Cam 1 (225 km/h | 140 mph).
Do Race 11 and after the finishline, turn immediately right and go hit Cam 1 (241 km/h | 149 mph).
Do Race 1 and Race 2.
Go back to SH and start Race 3. If you trigger the cops in the race, finish the race without losing them. If you don't, start a manual chase.
