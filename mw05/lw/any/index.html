Least Wanted Any% route

An optimized route that demands mastery, consistency, and flawless execution.
This route uses the Expert Career Any% route style, meaning everything will be shorten.

Before starting your journey, there are two things important to note :

  • The run ends at the end of Razor's chapter. You don't need to complete the final pursuit.
  • Your cash balance1.

1At Kaze, you want to switch to the Elise to save time. Otherwise, it requires a lot of money to upgrade (at least 84300$). Markers at the end of each boss are random and creates few scenarios, hence the need to draw a table.

To not encounter any issue during a run, you need to get Sonny's car and the 10k cash marker, otherwise you won't get enough cash on Kaze to upgrade the Elise. Not having any cash or car markers basically means a RESET.

Every scenario are viable to fully upgrade the Elise on Kaze. However there is only one scenario that isn't viable for Webster upgrades (marked in red in the second table).
You will miss 2000 $ and two options opens themselves to you then. Once at Webster's chapter, skip Ultimate NOS or RESET.

Prologue​

Lapglitch C1, C2, C3 and C4.

I sure hope you like watching the cutscene.
Complete the prologue. After Rog circuit, go back to SH and enter career to skip the Razor cutscene.
Buy Golf and drive to SH. Pressing the Reset button while in the Loading screen can save you time, up to 0.3-0.4 second.

Sonny​

Do Race 1. Then, turn around and go to the Performance Shop.
Upgrade Race Engine and install Reaper spoiler.
Do Race 2. Don't forget the Aero +5 tune at the stadium.
Do Race 3 then challenge Sonny. Choose 2 random markers.

Vic​

Upgrade Race Transmission, Pro Suspension, Pro NOS and Pro Tires.
Lap glitch KO3 and KO4.
Do Race 1. After the 3-2-1 countdown, tune Handling -1 and Ride Height +5.
Do Race 2, Race 3 and Race 6.
Challenge Vic. Choose the Trans junkman marker and 1 random marker.

Big Lou​

Upgrade Pro Engine.
Do Race 1, Race 3, Race 6, Race 7 and Race 8.
Lapglitch C10 and challenge Big Lou. Choose the Tires junkman marker and 1 random marker.

Remark​

*After beating Big Lou, you can choose one random marker to at least get his car or the cash marker and THEN choose the Junkman Tires marker. * You can also go for two random markers to be fully sure to get at least one important marker and pick the Tires junkman one at Kaze.

Earl​

Lapglitch C14.
Do Race 1, Race 2, Race 4, Race 7 and Race 8.
Challenge Earl. Choose the Turbo junkman marker and 1 random marker.

Kaze​

Choose Kaze cam 2 and go to the Car Lot to buy the Elise.
Go back to SH and sell all other cars.
Lapglitch KO10.

Choose Jewels cam 1, go to the Performance shop and upgrade Pro Engine, Pro Transmission, Super Pro Suspension & NOS, Ultimate Tires, Super Pro Turbo and all the junkman parts you have. Install any spoiler.

You could install Pro Brakes to improve the car steering a bit but only if you have enough cash. The performance part costs 5200$.

Do Race 1, Race 3, Race 5, Race 6, Race 8, Race 9 and Race 10.
Challenge Kaze. Choose either the Tires junkman marker and 1 random marker OR 2 random markers.

Webster​

Upgrade Super Pro Engine & Transmission and Ultimate Supsension & NOS.
Lapglitch C18.
Do Race 1, Race 2, Race 4, Race 5, Race 7, Race 8 and Race 10.
Challenge Webster. Skip the markers.

Ronnie​

Lapglitch C21 and KO16.
Do Race 1, Race 2, Race 3, Race 5, Race 6, Race 8, Race 11 and Race 12.
Challenge Ronnie. Skip the markers.

Razor​

Upgrade Ultimate Engine, Transmission & Turbo.
Do Race 1, Race 2, Race 3, Race 4, Race 5, Race 8, Race 9, Race 10 and Race 11.
Challenge Razor. The cutscene of his first Boss race is skippable.

Congratulations ! You just did a wonderful run (or got lucky who knows...).
I guess it's time to start over and improve your race times. Good luck ! 😉
